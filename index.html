About Oze Photography

Oze Photography is a creative photography brand dedicated to capturing real moments, genuine emotions, and timeless memories. Based in Rwanda, Oze Photography focuses on delivering high-quality images that tell meaningful stories through light, composition, and detail.

Driven by passion and creativity, Oze Photography specializes in portrait, lifestyle, and event photography. Every project is approached with care, professionalism, and a strong artistic vision to ensure each image reflects authenticity and beauty.

At Oze Blaise Photography, we believe every image should speak for itself. With a professional approach and creative eye, we help clients capture memories that last a lifetime. Whether it’s portraits, celebrations, or branding, we deliver stunning visuals that reflect who you are and what matters most to you.
